Inspired by the Greats

Path of Exile (1) stands proudly on the shoulders of Diablo II — borrowing its dark tone, itemization depth, and brutal gameplay loops. However, PoE took that inspiration and built something far grander.

Why PoE is the Best

  • Massive passive skill tree with thousands of builds
  • Deep item crafting and economy-based trading
  • Seasonal leagues with new mechanics every few months
  • No pay-to-win elements — skill and time define mastery
  • Gritty, atmospheric world true to the ARPG spirit

Diablo II’s Spirit Lives On

Path of Exile recaptures that magic feeling of exploration, danger, and discovery — all while staying true to the principles that made Diablo II legendary.

“PoE isn’t just inspired by Diablo II — it’s its spiritual successor.”
